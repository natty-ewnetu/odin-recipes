shiro wot

shiro wot is a thick, flavorful Ethiopian stew made from spiced chickpea flour(shiro), simmered with onions, garlic and berbere spice, typically served with injera.

ingredients

  • shiro powder
  • onions
  • garlic
  • barbere spice
  • oil or niter kibbeh
  • water
  • salt
  • tomato(optional)
  • green chili(optional)

steps

  1. heat oil or niter kibbeh in a pot over medium heat
  2. add chopped onion and saute until soft and translucent
  3. add minced garlic (and tomato if using), cook for a few minutes.
  4. stir in berbere spice and cook briefly to releas aroma.
  5. gradually add water, stirring continuously.
  6. whisk in shiro powder slowly, stirring continuously.
  7. simmer on low heat, stirring often, until thick and smooth(10-15 minutes).
  8. season with salt to taste.
  9. serve hot withinjera or flatbread
