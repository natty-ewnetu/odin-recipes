atikilt

Atikilt is a mild Ethiopian vegitable stew made with cabbage, potatoes, and carrots, gently spiced with turmeric and served with ingera.

ingredients

  • cabbage
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • garlic
  • oil
  • turmeric
  • salt
  • green chili(optional)
  • black pepper(optional)
  1. heat oil in a pot and saute onionsandgarlic until soft.
  2. Add turmeric (a green chili if using), stir well.
  3. Add chopped carrots and potatoes, cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add shredded cabbage and a little water.
  5. cover and simmer until all vegitables are tender.
  6. Add salt (and black pepper if using) to taste.
  7. serve warm with ingera or bread.
