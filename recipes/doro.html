doro wot

doro wot is a spicy Ethiopian chicken stew with onions, berbere, and boiled eggs, served with ingera.

ingredients

  • chicken
  • Onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • berbere spice
  • niter kibbeh(Ethiopian spiced butter)
  • food oil
  • salt
  • hard-boild eggs
  • water
  1. Marinate chickenwith lemon juice and salt; set aside
  2. slow-cook chopped onions (without oil) until soft and golden.
  3. Add niter kibbeh, then stir in garlic, and berbere spice; cook until fragrant
  4. Add a littel water to make a thick sauce.
  5. Add a marinated chicken; cook covered until tender.
  6. Add boiled eggs and simmer to absorb the flavors.
  7. adjustsalt and serve hot with
